Personality Section

Aries signs tend to have a lot of energy, they apply this energy to every aspect of their lives. Very confident and likes to "do first, ask questions later". Not surprisingly since they are the zodiac’s first sign, being number-one is something they strive for and tend to betrendsetters and trailblazers. Aries are true originals and the ulitmate cheerleaders. Their fire power is present in their optimistic attitude there’s nothing an Aries can’t/won’t take on. At times, Aries can be selfish or overly focused on themselves. Aries are born with an innate sense of entitlement, which helps them shatter glass ceilings but can also be off-putting to people in extreme doses. This go-getter sign can come across as abrasive or overly aggressive. However, Aries will never back down from a challenge and can be heroic champions of anyone in distress.
